ABOUT US

Welcome to CODENET, a creative initiative by a passionate group of computer engineering undergraduates. CODENET is more than just a website. It's a simple, user focused solution powered by BALLERINA coding language to solve a real life problem. As students we aim to bridge the gap between day-to-day issues and technology by building something meaningful, minimal and practical. With codenet, we strive to turn our fundamental coding skills into impactful innovation.
